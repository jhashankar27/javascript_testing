About Our Temple

Our History

Founded in 1950, our temple has been serving the community for over seven decades. We are dedicated to preserving and promoting Hindu culture and spirituality.

Our Mission

To provide a sacred space for worship, spiritual growth, and cultural education while fostering a sense of community among devotees.
